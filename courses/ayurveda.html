What is AYUSH? (Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, Homoeopathy) üåø

**AYUSH** is an acronym for the traditional and non-conventional systems of medicine in India: Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homoeopathy. These systems of medicine are gaining popularity for their holistic and natural approach to health.

Think of it as a career in natural healing. If your child is interested in a holistic approach to medicine, is curious about traditional remedies, and wants to help people live a healthier, more balanced life, an AYUSH degree is a great fit. It is the **foundation** for a career in alternative medicine.

Key AYUSH Systems and Degrees üçÉ

The different systems of medicine under AYUSH have their own undergraduate degrees:

BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery): A five-and-a-half-year course that focuses on the ancient principles of Ayurveda.

BHMS (Bachelor of Homoeopathic Medicine and Surgery): A five-and-a-half-year course on Homoeopathy, which involves treating illnesses with highly diluted substances.

BUMS (Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery): This course is based on the Unani system of medicine, which originated in ancient Greece.

BNYS (Bachelor of Naturopathy & Yogic Sciences): A four-and-a-half-year course that uses natural therapies like yoga, diet, and herbal medicine to treat diseases.

BSMS (Bachelor of Siddha Medicine and Surgery): This course is based on the Siddha system, which originated in ancient South India.

These courses are a blend of traditional knowledge and modern science.

AYUSH doctors have many opportunities in the healthcare sector, especially with the government's support for these fields. Here are some of the most common career paths your child can pursue:

Private Practice: Many AYUSH doctors open their own clinics and practice their form of medicine.

Government Jobs: The government hires AYUSH doctors for various roles in public health and research.

Medical Tourism: India is a popular destination for medical tourism, and many AYUSH doctors work in this sector.

Health & Wellness: AYUSH doctors can work in wellness centers, spas, and resorts, promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Academics & Research: With a master's degree, AYUSH doctors can become professors or researchers in their field.
